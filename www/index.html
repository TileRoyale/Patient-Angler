Market

Sell your caught fish here.

Only fish take up storage space. Plants and trash are discarded automatically and do not count toward your storage limit.

Guild Orders — each week the Fishermen's Guild posts a supply order for specific fish.

Catch the required fish and sell them on the Market. Progress updates automatically as you sell.

Complete the full order before the week ends to earn a coin bonus reward.

★ Golden Contracts appear occasionally and offer a larger reward for rarer fish.

1 real-life hour = 1 in-game day, so a guild week lasts 7 real hours. Only manually caught fish count toward orders — automation does not contribute.

Sunken Treasure Chests appear in your Market when discovered.

Chests can be found by: manual fishing (rare chance), your automation fleet, or Expedition Vessels (guaranteed 1 chest per 30 in-game days each).

Each chest contains Diamonds and a large coin reward based on your current automation income. Ocean-tier chests offer bigger rewards.

Sell All does not open chests — tap Open on each chest individually. Chest capacity starts at 1 and can be expanded via the Treasure Hold upgrade in the Pearl Shop.

How Competitions Work

Join a competition and catch the highest-value fish within 5 minutes. You compete against 9 other anglers.

Your best single catch is your score — only that fish counts, not total catches.

Earn coin rewards based on your final rank. Top 3 earn the biggest prizes.

Competitions run in a series of 10 rounds. After all 10, a Grand Winner is crowned based on total series performance.

Only manually caught fish count — automation does not contribute.

Fishdex & Mastery

Catch each fish, plant, or trash item to unlock its Fishdex entry. Catching the same species repeatedly builds up its Mastery.

Mastery tiers per species:

Bronze — 100 catches (1 pt)
Silver — 1,000 catches (2 pts)
Gold — 10,000 catches (3 pts)
Platinum — 100,000 catches (4 pts)
Diamond — 1,000,000 catches (5 pts)

Mastery points accumulate per zone. Reaching point thresholds unlocks permanent zone bonuses — better sell prices, faster automation, higher rare fish chances, and more.

Mastery is never reset, even after Prestige.

Targeted Lure (available in Bait shop) lets you mark specific fish as targets. Targeted fish appear more often in your catches.

Each Targeted Lure level gives you one extra target slot. Open any caught fish in the Fishdex and tap Target to add it. Tap again to remove it.

Limit reached means all your target slots are full — remove a target first to add a new one.
