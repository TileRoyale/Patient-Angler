4. Critical Mismatches

CRITICAL F-01 — Tribe Request formula calibrated to Abyss speed, tracking Overworld catches
Formula: totalCatches = zone.expectedCatchsPerSecond × 3 × 86400
Zone 1 expectedCatchsPerSecond = 10,000 → 2,592,000,000 total catches for Initial Request.
But tribe catches are tracked via onCatchEvent() → recordTribeFishCatch(), which fires from Overworld automation only. Abyss automation does not call this path.

Max realistic Overworld production targeting Zone 1 species (tuna, swordfish, marlin in Ocean; salmon in Sea):
5 DSF in Ocean = 50 catch/s × 79.2% fish fraction = 39.6 fish/s. Tuna (common) ≈ 1/5 of common pool → ~5.6 tuna/s.
Tuna needed: 777,600,000 ÷ 5.6 = 138,857 days for tuna alone.
Even with 20 DSF in Ocean: ~555,000 days. Design target: 3 days.
Discrepancy: ~2,000–50,000×.
CRITICAL F-02 — Zones 9 & 10 require Legendary fish that automation cannot catch
rollCatch(zone, false) strips type === 'legendary' from loot table (game.js:2636). Confirmed.

Zone 9 (Sapphire Deepwatch) Initial Request:
• giant_squid: Legendary, time-locked 00:00–02:00 in-game (= 5 real minutes per hour) → 9,331,200,000 needed
• coelacanth: Legendary, time-locked 01:00–03:00 → 4,665,600,000 needed
At 6h/day active fishing in Ocean with Legendary = 4% ocean catch rate (manual): ~10 Legendary/hour × 6h × 1/3 giant_squid = 20/day.
9.3B ÷ 20/day = 466,560,000 days (~1.3 million years).

Zone 10 (Blue Diamond Ancients): requires giant_squid (5.18B) = same impossibility.
CRITICAL F-03 — Abyss automation output is 50–100× below design targets
expectedCatchsPerSecond in ABYSS_ZONES (10k–100k) was used to size Tribe requests but the Abyss automation batch in Phase 9 derives from Overworld automation capacity × Abyss zone fraction.

Calculation: With 2 active slots (1 OW, 1 Abyss), Abyss gets 50% of total OW automation output.
Max late-game automation: 5 DSF × 6 zones × 10/s × speed multipliers ≈ 300–450 catch/s total.
Abyss fraction (50%): ~150–225 catch/s actual Abyss output.
Design target for Zone 1: 10,000 catch/s. Actual: ~200/s. Gap: 50×.
Zone 10 design target: 100,000 catch/s. Actual: ~200/s. Gap: 500×.
CRITICAL F-04 — Abyss fish fill storage in seconds at design production speeds
Abyss fish go to G.abyss.abyssFishPile, which counts toward storageCapacity(). When full, ALL automation pauses.

At Zone 1 design speed (10,000/s): 10,000 × 60% fish = 6,000 fish/s.
Harbor Cold Storage (max non-Tribe): 10,000 capacity → fills in 1.67 seconds.
At actual Phase 9 rates (~200/s): 200 × 60% = 120 fish/s → fills in 83 seconds.
Player must manually open Market and sell Abyss fish to unblock automation.
No auto-sell mechanism exists for Abyss fish.
HIGH F-05 — Three Tribe bonus stats are defined but never applied
Getter functions exist in abyss.js but are not called anywhere in game.js:
• getTribeManualCatchSpeedMultiplier() → Ruby Forged bonus (up to +5%). Should reduce tap timing window or increase rod speed. Not called anywhere.
• getTribeRareChanceBonus() → Opal Gardeners (up to +1% rare/epic chance). Should feed into getRarityBonus() or rollCatch(). Not called anywhere.
• getTribeExpeditionSpeedMultiplier() → Topaz Riftborn (up to +10%). Defined but _getExpeditionSpeedMultiplier() at abyss.js:809 returns hardcoded 1 without calling it. Bypassed.
HIGH F-06 — calcFishRate() omits getTribeAutomationSpeedMultiplier()
autoTick() applies tribeAutoMult (up to 1.12 at Amethyst Seers Exalted).
calcFishRate() at game.js:4431 does not include this multiplier.
Result: HUD fish/s display is up to 12% lower than actual production. Also affects getEstimatedHourlyIncome() and Abyss auto-batch estimates that use calcFishRate() as a reference.
HIGH F-07 — buyTransport() uses hardcoded >= 2 instead of getActiveSlotLimit()
At game.js:~1459, buyTransport() auto-activates a newly unlocked zone when G.activeAutomationZones.length >= 2. Should use getActiveSlotLimit() so the third Diamond slot is respected. Currently, buying the third slot does not improve transport auto-activation behavior.
MEDIUM F-08 — Maelstrom golden crystal: 150 missions required (bottleneck)
Golden crystals needed: 15. Expected golden per mission: avg 2 (range 1–3). Golden probability: 5%.
Expected total missions to guarantee 15 golden: 15 ÷ 2 ÷ 0.05 = 150 missions.
At 1 vessel, 6h each: 150 × 6 / 24 = 37.5 days. At 2 vessels: 18.75 days. At 5: 7.5 days.
This is manageable but golden crystal distribution has high variance (5% weight). See Section 6 for full analysis.

7. Abyss Zone Timeline

Zone unlock path (abyss.js ABYSS_ZONES unlockRequirement):

  • Zone 1 (Emerald Forest): Complete Maelstrom stabilization → ~19–37 days
  • Zone 2 (Amber Reef): Complete Zone 1 Initial Request + catch Zone 1 Mythic

Because Tribe Initial Requests are 200–3,850× larger than achievable (F-01), all zones beyond Zone 1 are effectively gated behind years of Overworld automation running. Zone 2 would require ~600–3,000 real-world days of continuous play at current formula values.

Multi-Zone Catch Requirement

Cross-zone species: Zone 1 requires salmon (Sea zone) + tuna/swordfish/marlin (Ocean zone). The player must have automation running in BOTH Sea and Ocean simultaneously to progress all four species. With only 2 active OW zone slots and at least 1 allocated to Abyss, the player can have at most 1 OW zone running — making multi-zone tribe requests even slower.

onCatchEvent() Call Chain

Confirmed: automation at game.js:3316–3321 calls onCatchEvent(fishId, rarity) which at line 2140 calls recordTribeFishCatch(fishId, 1) for non-trash, non-plant catches. Tribe progress IS tracked from Overworld automation. Abyss automation (processAbyssAutoBatch()) does NOT call this chain.

Proportional Zone Split

Abyss fraction = 1 - (owZoneCount / totalActiveZoneCount) Example: 1 OW zone + 1 Abyss zone → Abyss gets 50% of total automation output. Example: 2 OW zones + 1 Abyss zone (3-slot) → Abyss gets 33%. Example: 0 OW zones + 2 Abyss zones → Abyss gets 100%. This split is consistent in autoTick() and calculateOfflineProgress(). ✓

calcFishRate() vs autoTick() Mismatch

autoTick() speedBase: getSpeedMult() × getPearlSpeedMult() × getMasteryAutoSpeedMult() × tribeAutoMult calcFishRate() per unit: getSpeedMult() × tm × getPearlSpeedMult() × getMasteryAutoSpeedMult() × getMultiCatch() ← tribeAutoMult MISSING At Amethyst Seers Exalted (+5% auto speed): calcFishRate() is 5% below actual. HUD fish/s counter and getEstimatedHourlyIncome() both underreport by this amount.

Offline Progress

Offline progress correctly applies getTribeOfflineIncomeMultiplier() (Aquamarine Tidefolk, max +5%), Tribe Storage multiplier via storageCapacity(), and Abyss zone fraction. The offline Abyss catch batch also calls _advanceAbyssGeodeAutoProgress(elapsedMs, ...). Offline cap remains the storage capacity limit.
