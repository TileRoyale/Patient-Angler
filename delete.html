You can request deletion of your account and all associated game data at any time.

Option 1 — Delete in-app

Open the game → Settings → Reset Progress. This immediately removes all locally stored game data from your device.

Option 2 — Request server-side deletion

To request deletion of any data stored on our servers (game progress, competition results, leaderboard entries), send us an email with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include your Google account name or player ID so we can locate your data.

Request Data Deletion
What gets deleted:
  • Game progress (coins, unlocked zones, caught fish)
  • Competition history and leaderboard entries
  • Any linked Google Play Games account association
Processing time: We will process your request within 30 days.
