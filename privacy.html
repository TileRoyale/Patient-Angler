Privacy Policy

Patient Angler: Idle Fishing
Developed by Recapsa OÜ / Henly Games
Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Patient Angler: Idle Fishing ("the Game", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The Game collects and processes the following data:

  • Game progress data — coins, unlocked zones, caught fish, settings, and other gameplay state. Stored locally on your device and, if you sign in with Google, also backed up to our secure cloud server (Railway / PostgreSQL).
  • Google account information — if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name, email address, and unique Google user ID solely for the purpose of linking your cloud save. We do not share this information with third parties.
  • Advertising ID — collected by Google AdMob for serving relevant advertisements. See Section 3 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your game progress across devices (cloud save)
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing
  • To prevent cheating and validate game state (server-side validation)

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — displays rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and advertising IDs to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In) — optional login for cloud save. If you sign in, your Google account information is processed by Firebase. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is never seen or stored by us. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Railway (cloud server) — hosts our backend for cloud save. Game state data is stored in a PostgreSQL database associated with your Google user ID. Data is stored in the EU.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is rated for users aged 13 and older and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data can be deleted at any time via Settings → Reset Progress within the Game, or by uninstalling the application. To request deletion of your cloud save data, contact us at the email below and include your Google account email. We will delete your data within 30 days.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted between the Game and our server is sent over HTTPS. Server-side anti-cheat validation is performed to maintain fair gameplay. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Game evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
